Manali – Adventure & Mountains

A beautiful hill town famous for snow, mountains, and adventure sports.

Plan Trip

• Best Time: October – February (snow), March – June (pleasant weather)

• Famous For: Solang Valley, Hidimba Temple, Rohtang Pass

• Activities: Paragliding, river rafting, trekking

• Entry Fee: Free

Shimla – The Queen of Hills

Known for colonial charm, pine forests, and scenic mountain views.

Plan Trip

• Best Time: April – June, December – January (snowfall)

• Highlights: Mall Road, Jakhu Temple, Christ Church

• Nearby: Kufri, Mashobra

• Entry Fee: Free

Dharamshala – Land of Peace & Monasteries

Home to the Dalai Lama, Tibetan culture, and serene mountain views.

Plan Trip

• Best Time: March – June, September – November

• Famous For: Monasteries, Bhagsu Falls, cricket stadium

• Nearby: McLeod Ganj, Triund Trek

• Entry Fee: Free

Kasauli – Quiet & Scenic Getaway

A peaceful town perfect for nature lovers and slow travelers.

Plan Trip

• Highlights: Monkey Point, Gilbert Trail, Christ Church
